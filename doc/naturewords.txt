For use with the code "Create 6IV Egg from Nothing (Grab ACE)"

This creates a hatchable Egg of any species, with 31 IVs in every stat in Box 9 Slot 27.
PID and OTID will both be 0x00000000, so Nature is always Hardy, and Ability is always 0.
However combined with FRLG Mail Glitch you can acheive any Nature/Ability combo.

Place the resulting Egg in Box 3 Slot 1 using the White Hand, and then enter the following words into the first four slots of the glitched Mail Message.

For example, entering:

ALL ALL
ALL ALL

Yields an Egg with:

PID: 0x102A102A
Nature: DOCILE
ABILITY: 0

After entering the words, you can hatch the Egg normally and it will have: 
Desire Species
31 IVs in all stats
Desired Ability/Nature
Legal Met Data (for breedable pokemon)
No Moves
No EVs
